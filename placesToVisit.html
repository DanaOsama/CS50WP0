Places to Visit

Explore the monuments of Egypt!

There are countless beautiful places in Egypt. Here, we list only a few of the main attractions and tourist hotspots.

The Giza Pyramids

The Giza Pyramids are surrouded by the mystery of how they were built. Go experience how sublime they truly are on a camel or a horse, and live in the past for a few precious moments!

The Nile River

The Nile River, flowing through nine countries, is the longest river in the world. Enjoy the Nile at night with the city lights glittering on its surface.

Sharm El-Sheikh

A city in the Sinai Peninsula with beautiful beaches and warm weather. There are so many activities you can do like snorkling to see the corals of the Red Sea and watching the beautiful sunset.

Abu Simbel Temples

Situated on the western bank of Lake Nasser, two massive rock temples carved out of the mountainside in the 13th century BC. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a trip back to the days of the Pharaoh Ramesses II.

The Citadel of Cairo

A glorious medieval Isalmic- era castle built by Salah ad-Din. Spend the day there and explore the rooms of the castle as well as the mosques and museums.

The White Desert

This white surfaced desert is widely known for its rock formations, overnight camping, and safari trips. Because it resembles the surface of a foreign planet, it was used as the setting for many Sci-Fi movies, like Vin Diesel's Riddick.

Sultan Qalawun Mosque

Sultan Qalawun Mosque is unique in that it combines the architecture of European Churches and designs from the Islamic world, showcasing Egypt's spirit of Islam and Christianity.

The Temple of Philae on Agilika Island

Explore the temple where Isis, the goddess of motherhood and fertility was worshipped byt the Pharaos.

Alexandria

Explore the city founded (and named after) Alexander the Great. Visit its beautiful shores, explore Bibliotheca Alexandria, and enjoy its beautiful weather in all seasons.
