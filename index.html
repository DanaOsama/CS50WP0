Why Egypt?

Egypt is not a country we live in but a country that lives within us -Pope Shenouda III

Between the dunes of the desert and the waves of the ocean, one of the most popular civilizations thrived by the Nile River. Although they don't live now, their tombs and temples remain some of the most magnificent attractions to humanity today. Mysterious and majestic, those monumnets are scattered all around Egypt, calling to those who would like to delve into the pages of the past. However, Egypt isn't just about the Pharaos. There are so many things that you can see in Egypt like the Nile River, the Citadel of Cairo, Khan el-Khalili, Bibliotheca Alexandrina, and even the beautiful Coral Reef of the Red Sea!
